Updated Bootcamp Learning & Wellbeing Plan

Long-term Goal / Career Pathway

I am really interested in video games, 3D modelling and animation. A long-term goal is to work in the gaming or creative technology space. In the short term, my goal is to become a web developer, which aligns with my skills in visual design and interactive experiences.

My Strengths

  • Adaptable and open to new ideas
  • Persistent when problem-solving — I keep trying until it works
  • Pick up new concepts quickly when focused
  • Creative with strong visual thinking

My Challenges / Growth Areas

  • Delay asking for help, try to push through alone
  • Online communication still feels less natural than in-person
  • Can feel overwhelmed when workload stacks up
  • Need to manage time to avoid distraction

Workload & Help-Seeking Plan

If I am stuck for more than ~30 minutes, I will reach out on Discord or ask a tutor/peer. I will keep a daily plan with small achievable tasks and start early to give myself buffer time.

Communication Plan

I will contribute regularly in group discussions, check in when unsure, and keep communication open instead of waiting silently.

Wellbeing / Oranga Plan

What keeps me well:

  • Focused sessions up to ~90 minutes
  • Breaks when frustration or brain fog appears
  • Music for concentration
  • Movement and stretching during the day
  • Keeping a steady daily routine

What harms my wellbeing:

  • Trying to power through alone for too long
  • Comparing my progress to others
  • Skipping breaks
  • Letting stress snowball

Actions I will follow:

Action When
Focused work periods ~90 mins Throughout each day
Take a break when frustration hits Whenever needed
Move away from the desk before dinner Daily
Ask for help if stuck ~30 mins Any time
Start earlier to maintain buffer Most afternoons
Record one achievement End of each day

Support People

Classmates, tutors, and people in my life outside of bootcamp.
