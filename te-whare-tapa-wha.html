Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Taha Whanau (Family Wellbeing)

My family aren't in Wellington right now but I can Facetime my family to stay in touch

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

For me, mainly to keep a healthy amount of socialisation. My goal is to have a least one social gathering every week, which could be band practice, going to a gig, or playing a tabletop game. This is because isolation is very bad for your mental health, as we all found out during COVID. Also practcing mindfulness.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

I'm maybe a little weird but I find spirituality through human interaction and art. Exploring the human condition. So for me keeping up my creative pursuits is how I stay spiritualy connected.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and enviroment)

I have gotten into gardening in that last few years and I feel like growing things and keeping things alive connects me to the land. Also just going for a walk in a nearby forest. Just connecting with the ecosystem and learning how things are interconnected.
